Welcome

The Fryer Law Firm, P.C. is a real estate law firm located in the heart of Atlanta. Our Mission is to provide a positive closing experience for the parties who have entrusted their real estate transaction to our care.

Although our firm specializes in the intown Atlanta neighborhoods, we can close your real estate transactions throughout Georgia. To begin, please call (404) 240-0007 or send your title request to Ron Kite at rkite@fryerclosings.com.

History

The Fryer Law Firm was founded in 1997 by Clara Fryer to serve exclusively as a real estate closing practice for metro-Atlanta neighborhoods. The attorneys and staff bring together decades of closing experience, including expertise in the areas of condominium/covenant law, builder/new construction issues, lender representation, and title examination work.

In 1999, the Firm renovated the former Buckhead Bed and Breakfast building located in the Lenox Pointe development. Lenox Pointe is immediately accessible from I-85/75, GA-400 and major in-town surface streets and is the firm's main office. A state-of-the-art closing facility with conference rooms of varying sizes, the main location also serves as “back office” for the firm's satellite closing sites located in agents' real estate offices in Atlanta and Decatur.
